About Me

My Name is Nelson Aizan and I'm 25 years old. I studied quantity surveying at univeristy and finished with a 1st Class Degree. I also worked within the construction industry for 5 years as a Trainee Cost Manager and later being promoted to Assistant Cost Manager. I worked on various projects such as the Smart Motorway M6 J13-15 and High Speed 2 (HS2) to name a few.

I became interested in Front End Development through a friend, keen to know more I started doing some research and began learning more. This was the start of my Front End Development journey as I started to take online lessons and gradually developing my skills.

I play the guitar and the keyboard, I also enjoy mountain biking and table tennis. When I was younger I wanted to become an actor so I audition for a spot to be part of the National Youth Theatre which had only 400 spaces in the whole of UK. I was succesful and became part of the National Youth Theatre.
